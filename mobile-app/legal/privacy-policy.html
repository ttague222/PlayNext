Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2025

Watchlight Interactive ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our PlayNxt mobile application.

Key Points:
  • Sign-in is optional - you can use the app anonymously
  • We don't sell your data to third parties
  • You can delete all your data at any time
  • We only collect what's needed to provide recommendations
  • Free users see ads; premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience
  • Some links to game stores may be affiliate links

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you choose to sign in (optional), we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name from your Google or Apple account
  • Profile Photo: If provided by your sign-in provider

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the app, we automatically collect:

  • Session Preferences: Your selected time availability, mood, platform preferences, and play style for each recommendation session
  • Game Interactions: Which games you accept, mark as "already played," or skip
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and a unique device identifier for authentication
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about ads displayed and your interactions with them (collected by our advertising partner, Google AdMob)
  • Subscription Status: Whether you have an active premium subscription (managed through RevenueCat)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos or media files
  • Microphone or camera data
  • Browsing history outside the app

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Session preferences: To provide personalized game recommendations based on your available time, mood, and play style
  • Game interactions: To improve recommendations and avoid suggesting games you've already played or skipped
  • Account info: To sync your preferences and history across devices (only if signed in)
  • Device identifier: To authenticate API requests and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure sign-in (operated by Google)
  • Google Cloud Firestore: For storing preferences and history
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your data at any time through the app settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase/Google Cloud for infrastructure (bound by their privacy policies)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Portability

You can view your recommendation history in the app's History tab.

5.2 Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Clear History: Removes your game interaction history
  • Delete All Data: Permanently removes all your data including preferences, history, and account information

Both options are available in Settings > Privacy & Data.

5.3 Opt-Out

You can use the app without signing in. Anonymous users get the same core functionality - sign-in only adds cross-device sync.

6. Children's Privacy

PlayNxt is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. International Users

For EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability

Our legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (providing the service) and consent (for optional sign-in).

For California Users (CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

8. Advertising

PlayNxt displays advertisements to free users through Google AdMob. Ads are shown approximately every third recommendation reroll. Premium subscribers do not see ads.

8.1 How Ads Work

  • We use Google AdMob to serve rewarded video advertisements
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and interaction data to serve relevant ads
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings

8.2 Opting Out of Personalized Ads

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Note: Opting out does not eliminate ads; you will still see non-personalized advertisements.

9. Affiliate Links

PlayNxt may include affiliate links to game stores and subscription services. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

9.1 Affiliate Programs

We participate in affiliate programs including:

  • Amazon Associates: Links to games on Amazon
  • Other affiliate programs may be added in the future

9.2 What This Means for You

  • Affiliate links do not affect the price you pay
  • We do not receive any personal information about your purchases
  • Affiliate partnerships do not influence which games we recommend

10. Third-Party Services

The app uses these third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Privacy Policy
  • Google Cloud: Privacy Notice
  • Google AdMob: Privacy Policy - For serving advertisements
  • RevenueCat: Privacy Policy - For subscription management
  • RAWG API: For game cover images (no personal data shared)
  • Amazon Associates: Privacy Notice - For affiliate links

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification for material changes (if you're signed in)
